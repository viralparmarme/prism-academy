Our Mission

Prof Rishab Rathod, founder of Prism Academy, is a strong believer of the sayings “Knowledge is Power" and “A Pen is mightier than a Sword”.

To realise his vision of making learning fun and engaging for kids, the teachers at Prism Academy have adopted application based teaching approach. They believe that laying a strong academic foundation is the stepping stone to a child’s bright future.

The academy believes that every child if given the right guidance can achieve the greatest heights of success. The academy focuses on technical nuances required to mould kids of today’s generation.



Our Speciality

We provide great Learning

EXPERIENCED FACULTY

In our mission to provide the best to our students we select only the best teachers with a proven track record. All our teachers come with vast experience and extensive knowledge in their respective fields. They have thorough understanding of the syllabus, paper pattern and marking system across different boards.

PERSONALISED ATTENTION

Todays generation is full of distractions and procrastination. We make sure that all our students get personalised attention , if and when required, so that they don’t lose their interest in their respective subjects.

PRE-SCHEDULED STUDY PLANNER

In todays world kids are constantly juggling between academics, extra-curricular activities and their time with friends and family. In order to keep up with this fast paced life, time management and planning are key. Our scheduled sessions along with customised study plans help kids tackle this situation and makes them a lot more efficient.


WEEKEND TESTS

Kids undergo weekly tests followed by doubt-solving and review sessions. Our sessions help maintain a healthy competitive environment amongst kids and increases their drive to perform better with each passing week.

WELL CURATED STUDY MATERIAL

Our specially curated study material with a compilation of test papers from different sources saves kids the stress and hassle of endless browsing and research.

TECHNOLOGY AIDED TEACHING

We make use of the latest technology (edmodo, google classroom, youtube) to make teaching more interesting.
